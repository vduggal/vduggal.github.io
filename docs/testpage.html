I am the very model of a modern Major-General,
I've information vegetable, animal, and mineral, I know the kings of England, and I quote the fights historical

From Marathon to Waterloo, in order categorical; I'm very well acquainted, too, with matters mathematical, I understand equations, both the simple and quadratical, About binomial theorem I'm teeming with a lot o' news, (bothered for a rhyme) With many cheerful facts about the square of the hypotenuse.
